📊 Key Findings for 2024

  • 1Electronics dominates at 51.5% of total revenue ($1.16M), driven almost entirely by Laptop Pro X ($916K). This single SKU represents 40% of all company revenue — a significant concentration risk.
  • 2Furniture is the #2 category ($693K, 30.8% share) with a healthy 57.8% margin. Standing Desk and Office Chair together contribute $613K, indicating strong demand for home/office workspace products.
  • 3June was the strongest month ($276K), more than 2.8× September ($99K), the weakest. This volatility suggests seasonality or campaign-driven spikes without a steady baseline.
  • 4All three sales channels perform almost equally (B2B: $750K, Online: $745K, Retail: $758K), showing healthy multi-channel diversification with no over-dependence on one channel.
  • 5North leads regions ($697K) but West ($624K) has the highest order count (262), suggesting West customers have smaller average order values — an upsell opportunity.
  • 6Overall profit margin is 54.8%, which is strong. Apparel has the highest margin (60.4%) while Appliances lags at 56.4%, but all categories are comfortably profitable.

🎯 Strategic Recommendations

🛡️ Reduce SKU Concentration Risk

Laptop Pro X drives 40% of revenue. Diversify by developing or marketing 2–3 additional high-ticket Electronics SKUs to reduce dependency on a single product.

📅 Smooth Out Revenue Seasonality

September dipped to $99K vs June's $276K. Launch promotions, subscription bundles, or B2B contracts in Q3 to fill the trough and stabilize cash flow.

🛋️ Double Down on Furniture

Furniture delivers 30.8% of revenue with 57.8% margins and growing WFH demand. Expand the product line and target corporate B2B deals for bulk office setups.

🌍 Upsell in West Region

West has the most orders (262) but lower avg order value. Introduce product bundles and cross-sell recommendations to increase basket size per transaction.

👗 Invest in Apparel Growth

Apparel has the best margin (60.4%) but only 7.7% revenue share. With proper marketing investment, it can scale profitably — highest ROI growth category.

🤝 Grow B2B Channel

B2B is nearly tied with Retail and Online. With dedicated account managers and volume pricing, B2B could become the dominant and most predictable revenue channel.
