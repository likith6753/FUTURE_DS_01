# FUTURE_DS_01
# Business Sales Performance Dashboard & Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business sales analysis designed to help organizations make data-driven decisions. Using a real-world sales dataset, the project focuses on identifying revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable categories, and regional performance through an interactive dashboard and business insights.

The objective is not only to visualize sales data but also to provide actionable recommendations that support business growth, improve profitability, and enhance strategic decision-making.



## Objectives

* Clean and prepare raw sales data for analysis.
* Analyze revenue and profit trends over time.
* Identify top-selling products and high-performing categories.
* Compare sales and profitability across different regions.
* Build a professional, interactive dashboard.
* Generate business insights and actionable recommendations.



## Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** (Dashboard Development)
* **Microsoft Excel** (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
* **Power Query** (Data Transformation)
* **Python (Optional)** for advanced analysis

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Profit Margin

## Dashboard Features

* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Profit Trend Analysis
* Top-Selling Products
* Category-wise Sales & Profit
* Regional Performance Analysis
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Discount vs Profit Analysis
* Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Year, Segment)

## Business Insights

* Identified the products contributing the highest revenue.
* Compared category-wise sales and profitability.
* Evaluated regional performance to identify growth opportunities.
* Analyzed monthly sales trends to understand seasonal demand.
* Examined the impact of discounts on overall profitability.


## Recommendations

* Increase inventory for high-performing products.
* Focus marketing efforts on profitable regions.
* Optimize discount strategies to improve profit margins.
* Promote high-margin product categories.
* Use sales trends for better inventory and demand forecasting.
